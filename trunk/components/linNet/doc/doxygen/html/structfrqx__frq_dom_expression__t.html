Detailed Description

A normalized expression in the frequency domain. It has a list of addends, the actual expression and a common factor, also an addend, which is used for normalization.
Different to an ordinary, denormalized expression, see frq_frqDomExpression_t, the "actual expression" has a defined range of powers. The individual powers of s and of the device constants are all positive and they are guaranteed to begin with the lowest power null.
